# Direction and collaboration interests

This file outlines directions in which the SGTA project could be extended,
particularly in collaboration with theory-oriented research groups.

## Theoretical directions

- Analysis of stability and convergence properties of the memory dynamics
  under simplified assumptions.

- Formal characterization of stationary regimes
  in low-dimensional representation spaces.

- Connections between emergent graph structure
  and known results in representation learning theory.

## Empirical directions

- Extension to additional tasks and modalities.
- Systematic study of capacity-limited regimes.
- Comparison with other forms of inductive bias and regularization.

## Collaboration interests

I am particularly interested in collaboration on:
- theoretical analysis of learning dynamics,
- geometric and dynamical systems perspectives on representation learning,
- principled evaluation beyond standard benchmark metrics.

The project is intentionally structured to allow both
analytical simplification and controlled experimentation.
